Card Solutions Delivered.

Innovative Card Solutions (“ICS”) powers the development and delivery of prepaid debit-based payment programs that capture the imagination of niche target groups. ICS provides an array of network branded prepaid cards that can be used to make purchases anywhere MasterCard® is accepted worldwide – even online. Our prepaid card programs give consumers a safe, easy-to-use alternative to make purchases, pay bills and get cash.

Innovative Card Solutions develops an incredible range of products, from GPR cards to gift cards, healthcare-related cards, payroll cards, and more, designed to meet the unique needs of consumers.

Our philosophy is that prepaid cards serve different purposes for different consumers. Our strategy is to develop successful prepaid card programs by segmenting the large, diverse underserved market and matching the marketing and distribution strategy chosen to the niche market segment identified.

Our mission is to develop unique co-branded prepaid card programs that are at the forefront of the electronic payments market evolution, by offering innovative products, leading edge designs and launching programs to coincide with special groups and organizations.

Our vision is to become the nation’s largest MasterCard® prepaid program manager by developing prepaid product innovations and technological applications that have brand visibility and the ability to capture untapped segments of the prepaid market.

Innovative Card Solutions, LLC is an independent privately held corporation with offices in Cincinnati and Chicago.
