Innovative's leaders are experienced professionals with diverse backgrounds in the financial services, technology, distribution, product development and marketing industry sectors. William G. Mays, Chairman

Serving as Chairman of Innovative Card Solutions, William Mays is President of Mays Chemical Company, headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana. Mays Chemical has over $200 million in sales and more than 190 employees, with facilities in Indianapolis, Chicago, Detroit, Cincinnati, Canada and Puerto Rico. The 21-year-old company is a fully integrated chemical distributor offering chemicals and other related raw materials, cleaning and sanitation products, outsourcing services, consolidation and materials management programs.

Mays Chemical has proven capable of doing business on a national basis with clients such as Eli Lilly, Abbott, DaimlerChrysler, Ford Motor, General Mills, General Motors, Kellogg U.S.A., Kraft Foods, Pharmacia, Philip Morris and Procter and Gamble. Mays Chemical has won numerous awards for excellence including the General Motors Outstanding Supplier of the Year award, given to the one percent of their top suppliers worldwide.

Mr. Mays serves on a wide variety of for-profit and non-profit boards and organizations. Currently he serves as a director of the boards of Anthem Insurance, Inc.; Bank One, Indiana; Indiana State Chamber of Commerce; Indiana University Foundation and Indiana University President’s Council; Indianapolis Chamber of Commerce; National Minority Supplier Development Council; National Urban League and United Way of Central Indiana. He is also involved with the National Commission on Entrepreneurship; United Negro College Fund; the National Alumni Giving Drive for the Consortium for Graduate Study in Management; and is co-chair of the annual Coca-Cola Circle City Classic. The list of honors and awards for Mr. Mays is lengthy.

William G. Mays received his B.A. in chemistry and master’s degree in business administration from Indiana University. He has received three honorary doctorates.

Damon A. Goins, President and CEO

As Chief Executive Officer for Innovative Card Solution, LLC, Damon guides the overall vision of the company and directs all aspects of an organization's policies, objectives, and responsible for the short- and long-term profitability and growth of the company.

Prior to Innovative Card Solutions, Damon was a Certified IT Project Manager (PMP) with IBM. For over two decades, Damon has had extensive experience in managing the information technology and processing systems for companies such as Electronic Data Systems (EDS), Xerox Corporation, as well as a consulting position with International Business Systems (IBM) Global Services Division. His accomplishments include the management of datacenter migrations and the Enterprise Resource Planning software for major clients including Sony Electronics, AT&T, Wisconsin Power and Light and a host of others.

Damon received a B.S. Degree in Computer Science and Information Technology from Kentucky State University.

Mathew B. Murphy III, Chief Financial Officer

Serving as Director of Financial Services for Innovative Card Solutions, Matt is the Director of Finance and Administration for Mays Chemical Company, Inc., one of the nation¹s largest chemical distributor. As a key member of its Senior Management Team, Mr. Murphy directs the administrative functions of the company’s ongoing acquisition and strategic planning efforts. Additionally, Matt provides daily financial oversight and management of the vast investment interests of the company’s owner, William G. Mays.

Prior to joining Mays Chemical in 1996 Matt served as Senior Vice President of Corporate Lending, Marketing, and Business Development at Huntington Bank, in addition to being a member of its Senior Loan Committee. He held similar positions in Commercial Lending at both Merchants National Bank and Midwest National Bank. During his 15-year tenure in the banking industry, he developed an extensive resume of financial expertise. Matt is a founding member and past treasurer of Indianapolis Urban Bankers; an appointee of the Lynx Capital Corporation Investment Committee.

In June 2005, Governor Mitch Daniels announced Matt’s appointment to the Board of Trustees of the Public Employee’s Retirement Fund of Indiana, the state’s largest public pension fund with assets in excess of $16 Billion. Admirable appointments by preceding Indiana Governors include; Director of the Indiana Health Facility Financing Authority, and appointment as Chairman & Trustee of the Indiana State Teacher’s Retirement Fund, the state’s second largest public pension fund.

Matt received a B.S. Degree in 1980 from Indiana University, Bloomington, majoring in Management & Administration and with a minor in Finance at the Kelley School of Business.

Wyatt Goins, Chief Operations Officer

As Chief Operations Officer for Innovative Card Solutions, Wyatt oversees day-to-day business operations, policies, objectives, and initiatives of the organization to ensure future growth. He is also responsible for the attainment of short- and long-term financial and operational goals.

Prior to joining Innovative Card Solutions, Wyatt’s career reflects 16 years of extensive marketing experience as a Managing Director for many high-profile customers including Fifth Third Bank, Sara Lee, Limitedbrands, Cincinnati Bell and International Paper.

Wyatt received a B.S. Degree in Business Administration and Marketing from Urbana University.

Jennifer Nenadov, Chief Technology Officer

Jennifer currently serves as the Chief Technology Officer for Innovative Card Solutions. As Chief Technology Officer, Jennifer manages and oversees all IT operations, including the real-time card authorization infrastructure and customer web access portals.

Prior to joining ICS, Jennifer played an integral role in the launch of over 20 custom card programs during her career by means of establishing and managing various Relationship Management, Project Management, and Card Solutions teams. Jennifer also worked at Fifth Third Bank in various Risk Management and Product Management roles. While at Fifth Third, Jennifer collaborated to launch several new card products - Paypass Debit Card, World MasterCard Credit Card, Secured Credit Card, and Health Savings Accounts.

Jennifer holds a B.S. in Accounting from the University of Akron and an MBA from Xavier University.
